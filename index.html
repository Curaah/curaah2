Most patients aren't careless.

🏥
Doctor gives a new prescription. Four medicines. Complex timings.

Patient goes home. Paper slip in pocket. Tries to remember everything.

How Curaah Helps

Not a reminder app.
A companion that knows you.

Every feature works together — building a complete picture of your health, so you never have to explain yourself from scratch again.

📸

Prescription Intelligence

Upload a photo of your prescription. AI extracts every medicine, dose, and timing — verified by a real pharmacist before it becomes your plan.

📦

Medicine Verification

Point your camera at any strip or tablet — Curaah confirms it's the right one. Especially built for elderly users who confuse medicines.

🤔

Understands "Why"

Skip a dose and Curaah doesn't just nag — it asks why, logs the real reason, and adapts your plan around your actual life.

⚠️

Drug Interaction Alerts

Seeing multiple doctors? Curaah flags dangerous medicine combinations they might not know about — and tells you to check.

📊

Lab Reports, Explained

Upload any test report. Curaah explains every value in plain Hindi or Punjabi, and shows your progress over time — not just numbers.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Dashboard

With your consent, family — even abroad — can see if medicine was taken, track progress, and get alerts. Real peace of mind.
