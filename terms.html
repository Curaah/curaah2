Section 01
What Curaah Is (and Is Not)

Curaah is an AI-powered health and medication adherence companion operated by Curaah HealthTech Pvt Ltd.

Curaah is:

  • A tool to organise your prescriptions, medicines, and lab reports in one place
  • An adherence tracker that reminds you and understands why doses get missed
  • An AI companion that explains your health information in plain language
  • A way for trusted family members to stay informed, with your consent
  • An emergency SOS tool to alert your chosen contacts quickly

Curaah is not:

  • A hospital, clinic, pharmacy, or licensed healthcare provider
  • A diagnostic or prescribing service
  • An emergency response service — Curaah's SOS notifies your own contacts; it does not replace calling 112 or local emergency services
  • A substitute for your doctor's medical judgment
In a medical emergency, call 112 (India) or go directly to the nearest hospital. Do not rely on Curaah's SOS feature alone in a life-threatening situation.

Section 04
Prescriptions & Medicines
  • When you upload a prescription, AI extracts medicines, dosages, and timings automatically
  • This extraction is reviewed by a verified pharmacist before it becomes your active medication plan, because handwritten prescriptions can be misread
  • You remain responsible for confirming your medication plan matches what your doctor actually prescribed
  • Medicine identification via photo (strip or tablet) is provided as a helpful check, not a guarantee — when in doubt, consult your pharmacist or doctor directly
  • Drug interaction alerts are informational flags, not a complete clinical safety check — always inform every doctor you see about all medicines you're taking
Section 05
