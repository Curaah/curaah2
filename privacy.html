In plain language: Curaah collects your health information — prescriptions, medicines, lab reports, and daily logs — to act as your AI health companion. AI helps read and explain this data, but a human pharmacist verifies anything that becomes part of your active treatment plan. We never sell your data. You can delete everything, anytime, from your account settings.
Section 01
Who We Are

Curaah is an AI-powered health and medication adherence companion, built and operated by Curaah HealthTech Pvt Ltd, a company based in Nangal, Punjab, India.

Curaah is a technology platform — not a hospital, clinic, pharmacy, or licensed healthcare provider. We help you understand, organise, and stay consistent with the care your doctor has already prescribed. We do not replace your doctor, and we never claim to.

For any privacy questions, contact us at curaahtech@gmail.com.

Section 03
How AI Processes Your Data

Curaah uses artificial intelligence to make your health data understandable and actionable. Specifically:

  • Prescription reading — AI extracts medicine names, doses, and timings from photos you upload. A verified pharmacist checks this before it becomes your active medication plan, since handwriting can be misread.
  • Lab report explanation — AI reads your uploaded reports and explains values in plain Hindi, Punjabi, or English.
  • Medicine identification — AI analyses photos of strips or tablets to identify the medicine and check it against your schedule.
  • The AI Companion chat — When you ask a question, AI uses a snapshot of your health profile (conditions, medicines, recent adherence) to give a relevant answer, without you needing to re-explain your history each time.

Your raw data is sent securely to our AI processing partners only through our backend servers — never directly from your device to a third party. We currently use Sarvam AI for Indian-language understanding, and large language model providers (such as Anthropic or Google) for reasoning over your health context, accessed only through our own secured server-side integration, never with API keys exposed publicly.

AI is not a doctor. Every AI response is accompanied by a clear disclaimer. AI-generated suggestions are general guidance, not diagnosis or prescription, and always direct you back to a qualified doctor for medical decisions.
Section 04
Why We Collect It
  • To build your Digital Health Twin — a profile that lets Curaah genuinely understand your situation over time
  • To track medication adherence — so reminders, follow-ups, and your Recovery Score reflect reality
  • To explain lab reports and medicines — in language you actually understand
  • To detect possible medicine interactions — especially when you see multiple doctors
  • To enable family caregiving — only for people you explicitly invite and approve
  • To respond in an emergency — SOS sharing only happens when you trigger it, and only with contacts you've allowed

We never use your health data for advertising, and we never sell it to insurers, pharmaceutical companies, or any third party.

Section 05
Who We Share It With
We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • Supabase — our database, authentication, and file storage provider (SOC 2 Type 2 certified). All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • AI processing partners — Sarvam AI and large language model providers, accessed only via our secured backend, used solely to generate the explanations and responses described above.
  • Verified pharmacists — to confirm AI-extracted prescription data before it becomes your active plan. Pharmacists see only the prescription needed for verification, not your full profile.
  • Family members you invite — only what you explicitly permit (see Section 6).
  • Emergency contacts — only if you trigger SOS, and only contacts you've marked to receive health data.

We will disclose information if legally required to do so by an Indian court or government authority, and will notify you where legally permitted.

Section 06
Family Member Sharing

Family sharing in Curaah is opt-in, granular, and fully reversible:

  • You choose who to invite — nobody gets access automatically
  • You choose exactly what they can see: medicines & adherence, lab reports, both, or emergency-only
  • Every grant and revoke is timestamped and logged for your own audit trail
  • You can revoke any family member's access instantly, at any time, from the Family Dashboard
Section 07
How We Protect Your Data
  • Encryption — all data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row Level Security — database-enforced rules ensure you can only ever access your own data, and family members only see what you've explicitly permitted
  • Private file storage — prescriptions, lab reports, and photos are stored privately and accessed only via short-lived signed URLs, never public links
  • Server-side AI keys — AI provider credentials are never exposed in app code; all AI calls run through our secured backend functions
  • Consent audit trail — every consent you give or revoke is permanently logged with a timestamp, for transparency and compliance
Section 08
Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — view all your data anytime from your Curaah dashboard
  • Correct — update inaccurate details yourself, or email us for help
  • Withdraw consent — turn off optional consents (family sharing, research, marketing) anytime from Privacy & Consent settings, without losing your account
  • Erase — request full account and data deletion; we complete this within 30 days
  • Data portability — request an exportable copy of your data
  • Grievance redressal — raise a concern and receive a response within 7 days

To exercise any of these, use the in-app Privacy & Consent page, or email curaahtech@gmail.com with your registered phone number.

Section 09
Data Retention
  • Account & profile data — retained while your account is active
  • Prescriptions & medicines — retained for continuity of care, up to 5 years, or until you delete them
  • Adherence & lifestyle logs — retained for 3 years to support trend analysis
  • AI conversation history — retained for 1 year, used to maintain conversation context
  • Consent & audit logs — retained for 7 years as required for compliance record-keeping

On account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days. Some anonymised, aggregated data (with no name, phone, or identifying detail) may be retained for service improvement, only if you separately consented to research use.

Section 10
Cookies

Curaah uses only essential session and authentication cookies needed to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising trackers, analytics pixels, or any third-party tracking cookies.

Section 11
Children's Data

Curaah accounts are intended for users 18 and older. If a parent or guardian wishes to manage a child's medication, they should do so under their own account and add the child's details within their own health profile, with appropriate care. Contact us if you believe a minor's data was collected inappropriately, and we will act promptly.

Section 12
DPDP Act 2023 Compliance
  • Explicit, granular consent — collected separately for health data processing, AI analysis, family sharing, research, and marketing — never bundled into one blanket agreement
  • Purpose limitation — data used only for the purposes described in this policy
  • Sensitive personal data classification — your health information is treated with heightened care as sensitive personal data under the Act
  • Data minimisation — we only collect what's genuinely needed for Curaah to function
  • Grievance officer — reachable at curaahtech@gmail.com for any data protection concern

As the Government of India finalises DPDP implementation rules, we will update our practices and this policy accordingly.

Section 13
Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users and the relevant authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in line with DPDP Act requirements, along with the steps we are taking to contain and resolve it.

Section 14

For changes that materially affect your rights, we'll notify registered users by email or in-app notice at least 7 days in advance. Continuing to use Curaah after an update means you accept the revised policy; if you disagree, you may request account deletion anytime.
